Our Story

Future Athlete Agency was created by a former international basketball player who’s been through the ups and downs of chasing a college basketball career in the U.S. We know how confusing the recruiting process can be—figuring out NCAA/NAIA rules, reaching out to coaches, and adjusting to a whole new environment. That’s why we’re here. Our goal is to guide international athletes through the recruiting process, helping them develop their skills, understand the system, and adapt to life both on and off the court. More than just getting players noticed, we focus on building confidence, overcoming challenges, and making sure every athlete has the tools to reach their full potential.

Our Services

Our vision is to bridge the gap between international talent and U.S. college sport programs, creating opportunities for athletes to showcase their skills, earn scholarships, and build successful careers on and off the court. Future Athlete Agency is your trusted partner in sports talent management. We specialize in talent player development, and contract negotiation to elevate the careers of aspiring athletes.
